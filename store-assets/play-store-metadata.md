### Full Description (4000 chars max)
```
Travelling with friends should be fun — not a spreadsheet.

ouiShare makes it effortless to track shared expenses, split costs fairly, and settle up with one tap. Whether it's a weekend trip, a group dinner, or a holiday abroad, ouiShare keeps everyone on the same page so you can focus on the experience.

**Add expenses in seconds**
Snap a photo of a receipt and ouiShare reads it for you — no manual entry needed. Or add expenses manually in any currency. Split equally, by percentage, or in custom amounts.

**See who owes what, instantly**
ouiShare automatically calculates the simplest way to settle up. No more awkward "who paid for what" conversations — the balance screen shows exactly what each person owes and to whom.

**Settle up your way**
Pay directly from the app by card (Stripe) or bank transfer (Open Banking). Or mark payments as settled manually if you pay in cash. ouiShare tracks it all.

**No account required to try it**
Join a trip as a guest with just your name — no email, no password. Create a Google or Apple account whenever you're ready to save your trips across devices.

**Built for groups**
Share a trip link and your friends join in one tap. Pre-add participants by email before they arrive so their expenses are already tracked when they sign up.

**Works offline**
Add expenses even without a connection. ouiShare syncs everything when you're back online.

---

ouiShare is free to use. Receipt scanning is included for new users.

Perfect for: holidays, weekend trips, group dinners, flatmate expenses, festivals, work retreats.
```
