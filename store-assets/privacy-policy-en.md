## 2. What Data We Collect and Why

### Account data
When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your **name** and **email address** from those providers. This is used solely to identify you within the app and to match you to trips you have been invited to join.

When you use the app as a guest, we store only the **display name** you provide.

### Trip and expense data
We store the trips, expenses, and payment records you create within the app. This includes expense descriptions, amounts, participant names, and settlement status. This data is stored in your account and is necessary to provide the core functionality of the app.

### Camera and photo library
When you use the receipt scanning feature, the app accesses your device camera or photo library to capture an image of a receipt. The image is sent to our secure processing service to extract expense details (amounts, descriptions), and is then stored in your account so you can view the original receipt alongside the expense at any time. Receipt images are stored in the EU and are deleted when you delete the associated expense or your account.

### Contacts
If you choose to add trip participants from your device contacts, the app reads contact names to help you. **Contact data is never uploaded to our servers** — it is read locally on your device only.

### Payment data
When you pay a settlement via card, the payment is processed directly by **Stripe**, our payment processor. ouiShare never sees or stores your card number, CVV, or full card details. Stripe is an independent data controller for payment data — see stripe.com/privacy.

When you use Open Banking (bank transfer), you are redirected to your bank's authorisation page via **Tink**. ouiShare does not access your bank credentials or account data directly.

### Technical data
We collect standard technical information necessary to operate the service, including your device type and operating system version. We do not use advertising identifiers and we do not run analytics tracking.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:

- **Contract performance (Article 6(1)(b))** — processing your name, email, and trip data is necessary to provide the service you signed up for.
- **Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f))** — technical operation and security of the app.
- **Consent (Article 6(1)(a))** — camera and photo library access, which requires your explicit permission at the device level.

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access** — you can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
- **Right of rectification** — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
- **Right of erasure** — you can ask us to delete your account and all associated data ("right to be forgotten").
- **Right of portability** — you can request your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Right to object** — you can object to processing based on legitimate interest.
- **Right to withdraw consent** — you can revoke camera/contacts permissions at any time via your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: **tyrtel@gmail.com**

We will respond within 30 days.
